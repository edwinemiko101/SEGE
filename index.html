About

Brief Overview.

SEGE SYSTEM AND CORPORATE RESOURCE COMPANY LIMITED was incorporated on the 11th of January 2008 as private limited liability company under the Nigerian Allied Matters Decree 1990. As an emerging global company, SSACRCL is committed to developing ideas and bringing innovation to the Oil and Gas sector in Nigeria and around the world, exploring opportunities particularly in project excecution and services within the sector. We operate with the perspective of complete dedication and protection of industrial institutions and facilities to ensure their on-stream availability.

Mission Statement.

To establish a trusthworthy, committed, efficient and reliable service as well as build a creative and diverse global team that will be committed to delivering quality-driven and satisfactory services.

Vision Statement.

To create inspiration and innovation in deliverable services, thereby making a positive impact in our communities and nation.

Policies

Safety & Quality Control Policies

In order to provide best quality services, we have developed a standard quality assurance policy and practice based on major internationally accepted standards. SSACRCL Management has been committed to safety awareness since its incorporation. We have recorded very minimal work injuries from 2008 until this day, while executing projects of hazardous nature and meeting project completion targets.

Our HSE department Organizes regular safety training programme and periodic safety meetings to increase our personnel's awareness of the need to work safe, act safe, wear Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) during job executions or during visits to site locations, recognize and avoid unsafe acts. As reinforcement, safety awards are accorded to workers with outstanding safety performances.
