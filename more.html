More About Us

SEGE SYSTEM AND CORPORATE RESOURCE COMPANY LIMITED.

At SEGE SYSTEM AND CORPORATE RESOURCE COMPANY LIMITED, we have a wide range of experience within the oil and gas sector, and other sub-sectors in the Nigerian economy in general and the Niger Delta region in particular. Over the years, SSACRCL has created and followed standard quality control/quality assurance policies, universally oriented safety policy, good community and industrial relations as can be evidenced from our successful handling of the various projects which up till date have been crisis free.
